What is Chilltouch?

Chilltouch got its start back in 2004 in the frozen tundra known as Wisconsin.

Chilltouch fuses multiple genres together like rap, wicked shit, and metal.

Chilltouch comprises of one constant member, Chronic, however he gets help from his bandmates. OJB, besides sharing the stage with Chronic, brings his heavy vocals and metal influences into the mix. Cyanide creates unforgettable guitar laced and electronic beats to bring tracks to the next level and also writes lyrics. Relic writes lyrics and also brings his vocal talents.
